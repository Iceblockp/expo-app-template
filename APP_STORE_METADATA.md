## App Store (iOS)

### Required Information

#### App Information

- **App Name**: Maximum 30 characters
  - Example: "Universal Expo Template"
- **Subtitle**: Maximum 30 characters (optional but recommended)
  - Example: "React Native Starter Kit"
- **Primary Language**: Select your app's primary language
- **Bundle ID**: Must match your app configuration
  - Example: `com.template.expotemplate`
- **SKU**: Unique identifier for your app
  - Example: `expo-template-001`

#### Category

- **Primary Category**: Choose the most relevant category
  - Examples: Productivity, Business, Utilities, Social Networking
- **Secondary Category**: Optional additional category

#### App Description

- **Promotional Text**: Maximum 170 characters (can be updated without review)
  - Highlight current features or promotions
- **Description**: Maximum 4,000 characters
  - Detailed description of your app's features and benefits
  - Include keywords naturally for App Store Optimization (ASO)
- **Keywords**: Maximum 100 characters (comma-separated)
  - Research relevant keywords for your app category
  - Example: "template,react native,expo,starter,mobile app"
- **Support URL**: Required
  - Example: `https://yourapp.com/support`
- **Marketing URL**: Optional
  - Example: `https://yourapp.com`

#### Version Information

- **Version Number**: Semantic versioning (e.g., 1.0.0)
- **Copyright**: Copyright notice
  - Example: "Â© 2024 Your Company Name"
- **What's New**: Maximum 4,000 characters
  - Describe new features and improvements in this version

### App Review Information

- **Contact Information**:
  - First Name
  - Last Name
  - Phone Number
  - Email Address
- **Demo Account**: If your app requires login
  - Username
  - Password
  - Additional instructions
- **Notes**: Any additional information for the review team
  - Special features to test
  - Known issues or limitations

### Age Rating

Answer questionnaire about:

- Violence
- Sexual content
- Profanity
- Gambling
- Horror/fear themes
- Mature/suggestive themes
- Alcohol, tobacco, or drugs
- Medical/treatment information
- Unrestricted web access

### Pricing and Availability

- **Price**: Free or paid (with price tier)
- **Availability**: Countries/regions where app will be available
- **Pre-order**: Optional pre-order availability

## Google Play Store (Android)

### Required Information

#### App Details

- **App Name**: Maximum 50 characters
  - Example: "Universal Expo Template"
- **Short Description**: Maximum 80 characters
  - Brief tagline for your app
  - Example: "Production-ready React Native starter template"
- **Full Description**: Maximum 4,000 characters
  - Detailed description with features and benefits
  - Use formatting (bold, italics, lists) for better readability
- **Package Name**: Must match your app configuration
  - Example: `com.template.expotemplate`

#### Categorization

- **App Category**: Choose primary category
  - Examples: Productivity, Business, Tools, Social
- **Tags**: Select up to 5 tags
  - Help users discover your app

#### Contact Details

- **Email**: Developer contact email (publicly visible)
- **Website**: Optional website URL
- **Phone**: Optional phone number

#### Privacy Policy

- **Privacy Policy URL**: Required if app collects user data
  - Example: `https://yourapp.com/privacy`

### Store Listing

#### Graphics Assets

- **App Icon**: 512x512 PNG (32-bit with alpha)
- **Feature Graphic**: 1024x500 PNG or JPEG
- **Screenshots**: See [Screenshots and Media](#screenshots-and-media)
- **Promo Video**: Optional YouTube video URL

#### Content Rating

Complete questionnaire about:

- Violence
- Sexual content
- Language
- Controlled substances
- Gambling
- User interaction
- Sharing of user information
- Sharing of physical location

### Pricing and Distribution

- **Price**: Free or paid (with price in local currencies)
- **Countries**: Select countries for distribution
- **Device Categories**: Phone, tablet, Wear OS, etc.
- **User Programs**: Beta testing, early access

## Screenshots and Media

### iOS Screenshots

Required sizes (at least one per device type):

#### iPhone

- **6.9" Display** (iPhone 16 Pro Max): 1320 x 2868 pixels
- **6.7" Display** (iPhone 15 Pro Max): 1290 x 2796 pixels
- **6.5" Display** (iPhone 11 Pro Max): 1242 x 2688 pixels
- **5.5" Display** (iPhone 8 Plus): 1242 x 2208 pixels

#### iPad

- **13" Display** (iPad Pro 6th gen): 2048 x 2732 pixels
- **12.9" Display** (iPad Pro 5th gen): 2048 x 2732 pixels

**Requirements**:

- PNG or JPEG format
- RGB color space
- Maximum 10 screenshots per device type
- Can include text overlays and device frames

### Android Screenshots

Required sizes:

- **Phone**: Minimum 320px, maximum 3840px
  - Recommended: 1080 x 1920 pixels (portrait) or 1920 x 1080 pixels (landscape)
- **7-inch Tablet**: Optional
  - Recommended: 1200 x 1920 pixels
- **10-inch Tablet**: Optional
  - Recommended: 1600 x 2560 pixels

**Requirements**:

- PNG or JPEG format
- Minimum 2 screenshots, maximum 8 per device type
- No alpha channel
- Can include text overlays and device frames

### App Preview Videos (iOS)

- **Duration**: 15-30 seconds
- **Format**: M4V, MP4, or MOV
- **Resolution**: Must match screenshot dimensions
- **Orientation**: Portrait or landscape (consistent with screenshots)
- **Maximum**: 3 previews per device size

### Promo Video (Android)

- **Platform**: YouTube
- **Duration**: 30 seconds to 2 minutes recommended
- **Format**: Any YouTube-supported format
- **Content**: Showcase app features and user experience

## Privacy Policy

### Required Elements

Your privacy policy should include:

1. **Data Collection**:
   - What data you collect (personal info, usage data, etc.)
   - How you collect it (forms, analytics, etc.)
2. **Data Usage**:
   - How you use the collected data
   - Who has access to the data
3. **Data Sharing**:
   - Third parties you share data with
   - Purpose of sharing
4. **Data Storage**:
   - Where data is stored
   - How long data is retained
5. **User Rights**:
   - How users can access their data
   - How users can delete their data
   - How users can opt-out
6. **Security**:
   - Measures taken to protect user data
7. **Children's Privacy**:
   - If app is directed at children under 13
   - COPPA compliance (US)
8. **Contact Information**:
   - How users can contact you about privacy concerns

## Tips for Approval

### iOS App Store

1. **Follow Guidelines**: Read and follow [App Store Review Guidelines](https://developer.apple.com/app-store/review/guidelines/)
2. **Complete App**: Submit a fully functional app, not a demo or beta
3. **Accurate Metadata**: Ensure descriptions match actual functionality
4. **Privacy Compliance**: Implement required privacy features
5. **Quality**: High-quality screenshots and app icon
6. **Testing**: Thoroughly test before submission
7. **Demo Account**: Provide working credentials if login required
8. **Response Time**: Respond quickly to review feedback

### Google Play Store

1. **Follow Policies**: Read and follow [Google Play Policies](https://play.google.com/about/developer-content-policy/)
2. **Quality Guidelines**: Meet [Core App Quality Guidelines](https://developer.android.com/quality)
3. **Target API Level**: Meet minimum target API level requirements
4. **Permissions**: Only request necessary permissions
5. **Content Rating**: Complete content rating questionnaire accurately
6. **Testing**: Test on multiple devices and Android versions
7. **Store Listing**: High-quality graphics and clear descriptions
8. **Updates**: Keep app updated with latest Android features

## Common Rejection Reasons

### iOS

- Incomplete or buggy app
- Misleading metadata or screenshots
- Privacy policy issues
- Inappropriate content
- Crashes or performance issues
- Guideline violations
- Missing required features (e.g., account deletion)

### Android

- Policy violations
- Inappropriate content
- Malware or security issues
- Intellectual property violations
- Misleading behavior
- Privacy policy issues
- Minimum functionality requirements not met
